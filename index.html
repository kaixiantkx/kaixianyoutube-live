      护持说明书A Note on Supporting This Practice

      写给可能在未来提供护持的人

      For anyone who may, in the future, consider offering support

      本说明书的目的，是让任何考虑提供护持的人，在做出决定之前，能够清楚了解这件事的全貌——而不是一时感动后才发现细节不符预期。

      This note exists so that anyone considering offering support can understand the full picture before deciding — rather than discovering mismatched expectations after an initial moment of goodwill.

      一、这是什么
      1. What this is

      这不是一个商业项目，也不是一个募资活动。这是一份个人修行的护持说明，写给可能在未来某个时间点，认同这件事的价值、并自愿想提供帮助的人。

      This is not a business project or a fundraising campaign. It is a note about supporting a personal practice, written for anyone who, at some point, may recognize its value and wish to help of their own accord.

      我目前在 YouTube 上直播动中禅（隆波田传承）的个人修行练习，并搭配隆波帕默尊者的"观心"教导。直播全程安静，不讲解、不开示，呈现的是真实、不剪辑的练习过程。这件事有三个并存的目的：督促自己长期、规律地坚持练习；留下完整的修行记录；以及寻找愿意支持这条修行之路的护持。

      I currently livestream my personal Mahasati (Luangpor Teean lineage) practice on YouTube, alongside Luangpor Pramote's mind-watching teachings. The stream is silent throughout — no narration, no teaching — showing the real, unedited process. This serves three purposes at once: sustaining long-term regular practice through accountability, keeping a record of the practice, and finding people willing to support this path.

      这份文件诚实地说明：我目前的处境是什么、这条路为什么走得通、护持具体会用在哪里、以及——同样重要的——你不会得到什么。

      This document explains honestly: my current circumstances, why this path is workable, exactly where support would go, and — just as important — what you would not get.

      2. My current circumstances
      背景
      Background

      I hold a Bachelor's degree (Honours) in Business Mathematics, with a technical background spanning automation (RPA), computer vision, OCR, and SAP data processing, with hands-on experience in enterprise automation. I'm currently in a career transition, actively job hunting in automation/AI roles while maintaining regular practice.

      修行年限
      Years of practice

      依循隆波田、隆波帕默传承练习动中禅近五年，期间也曾有短期出家及内观禅修营的经验。这不是一时兴起的尝试，是经过五年验证、确认能长期坚持的修行方式。

      I've practiced Mahasati in the Luangpor Teean and Luangpor Pramote lineage for nearly five years, including a short-term ordination and a vipassana retreat. This isn't a recent whim — it's a method verified through five years of sustained practice.

      财务现况
      Financial situation

      目前有个人存款，可支撑一段时间的基本生活所需；同时有家人提供的过渡期协助，作为额外缓冲，而非长期依赖。这意味着：护持不是这件事能否继续的先决条件，而是锦上添花。这一点很重要——它确保了即使暂时没有任何护持出现，修行与直播本身也能持续，不会因为"等不到资助"而被迫中断。

      I have personal savings covering a period of basic living costs, plus family support as an additional buffer rather than a permanent dependency. This matters: support isn't a precondition for this to continue — it's a welcome addition. Even with no support at all, the practice and livestream continue.

      三、为何是这条路
      3. Why this path

      我曾认真评估过出家这条路，包括实地了解寺院生活、短期出家体验。最终判断在家修行更适合我目前的现实状况，原因有二：

      I seriously weighed ordination, including direct exposure to monastery life and a short-term ordination experience. I concluded lay practice better fits my current reality, for two reasons:

      1. 身体状况——我有裂孔疝（hiatal hernia），无法配合过午不食的戒律，长时间空腹会加重胃酸反流；
      2. Physical condition — I have a hiatal hernia, making the no-food-after-noon precept unsustainable; prolonged fasting worsens acid reflux;
      3. 生活方式——寺院日常的法会与接待活动，对我的身心状态而言负荷过高，长期处在高频互动环境中是消耗而非滋养。
      4. Lifestyle — the frequent ceremonies and hosting duties of monastery life are more than I can sustain; prolonged high-interaction environments deplete rather than nourish me.

      公开直播，则是借助"被记录、被看见"这件事，为长期坚持练习增加一层外部督促力，同时让这件事被更多人知道——这两者结合，才有了"护持"这个可能性的存在基础。

      The public livestream uses accountability — being recorded, being seen — to help sustain long-term practice, while also letting more people know about it. Together, these create the basis for the possibility of support.

      如果想放弃的话If You Want to Give Up

      给那个想停下来的自己

      For the self that wants to stop

      如果你正在读这一段，说明某个念头已经生起了。先知道它生起，不急着回应。

      If you're reading this, a thought has already arisen. Just know that it arose. No need to respond yet.

      一、念头只是念头
      1. A thought is just a thought

      "想放弃"是一个念头，不是事实，也不是命令。

      "I want to give up" is a thought — not a fact, not a command.

      动一下手，知道自己在动。这个念头来了，知道它来了——就像每一个曾经生起又灭去的念头一样。

      Move your hand, know that you're moving. This thought has come — know that it came, just like every thought before it that rose and passed.

      不需要现在就决定要不要听它的。

      You don't need to decide right now whether to listen to it.

      二、记得为什么开始
      2. Remember why you started

      近五年了。不是这几个月才决定的事。

      Nearly five years. This wasn't decided a few months ago.

      出家这条路认真想过、实地了解过，最后发现在家修行才是自己真正能走下去的路——不是因为退缩，是因为诚实地看清楚了身体和心的限制，然后选了一条真实可行的路，而不是一条听起来更崇高但自己撑不住的路。

      You seriously considered ordination, looked into it directly, and found that lay practice was the path you could truly sustain — not retreat, but an honest look at the body's and mind's limits, choosing what's real and workable over what merely sounds more noble.

      直播这件事，本质上是"督促自己"的工具，不是目的本身。工具可以累，目的不会变。

      The livestream is, at its core, a tool for self-accountability — not the goal itself. A tool can get tiring. The goal doesn't change.

      三、辛苦是真的，但不等于错
      3. The hardship is real, but it's not the same as being wrong

      镖头开着会累，长时间保持节奏会累，等不到回应会累，这些都是真的，不需要否认或压下去。

      The camera being on is tiring. Keeping the rhythm for long stretches is tiring. Waiting without response is tiring. All of this is real — no need to deny or suppress it.

      但"这件事很累"和"这件事是错的"，是两件不同的事，不要混在一起。累，知道累，继续动。

      But "this is tiring" and "this is wrong" are two different things — don't mix them. Tired, know that you're tired, keep moving.

      隆波田说的自然、不刻意，也包括不刻意逼自己撑住——累了就休息，但休息不是放弃，是这条路本来就会有的节奏。

      Luangpor Teean's naturalness, his ease without force, includes not forcing yourself to push through. Rest when tired — rest isn't giving up, it's the natural rhythm of this path.

      四、护持没出现，不代表这件事没价值
      4. No support showing up doesn't mean this has no value

      如果想放弃的念头，是因为"等不到人来护持"——先把这两件事分开看。

      If the urge to quit comes from "no one has offered support yet" — separate these two things first.

      督促自己练习，留下修行记录，这两个目的从第一天就完全独立于有没有人资助。它们没有因为没人资助而失败，它们本来就不需要任何人来验证。

      Self-accountability and keeping a record were independent of support from day one. They haven't failed for lack of funding — they never needed anyone's validation to begin with.

      有缘人，意思就是不强求。等待本身不是失败的证据。

      "The right person, in time" means not forcing it. Waiting itself is not evidence of failure.

      五、记得更大的方向
      5. Remember the larger direction

      这一切練習，最终指向的不是订阅数、不是护持金额，是灭苦，是证果，是涅槃——这是真正不会因为任何外境（有没有人看、有没有人资助、今天状态好不好）而动摇的依靠。

      All of this practice ultimately points not to subscriber counts or donation amounts, but to the cessation of suffering, to realization, to nibbāna — a refuge that truly cannot be shaken by any external condition: viewers or none, support or none, a good day or a bad one.

      外面的事情会起会落，直播会有人看也会没人看，求职会有回应也会没回应——这些都是无常的，本来就会变化。但觉知本身这件事，每一次"知道"，都已经是完整的，不需要等到任何外在结果出现才算数。

      External things rise and fall — viewers come and go, job applications get answered or don't. These are impermanent by nature. But awareness itself, each act of "knowing," is already complete — it doesn't need any external result to count.

      如果今天什么都没有发生、什么都没有改变——只要今天还有觉知到一个动作，这一天就不是空过的。

      If nothing happened today, nothing changed — as long as one movement was known today, the day was not wasted.

      六、如果真的撑不住了
      6. If you truly can't go on

      暂停不是放弃。调整时段、缩短直播、甚至下播休息几天，都可以。

      Pausing is not giving up. Adjusting hours, shortening streams, even taking days off — all of this is allowed.

      唯一不需要做的事，是因为一时的疲惫或挫折，就把这五年的修行和这件事本来的价值，全部一笔推翻。情绪会过去，不要在情绪最浓的当下做最终的决定。

      The one thing not to do: let a moment of fatigue or frustration erase five years of practice and its inherent worth. The feeling will pass — don't make final decisions in its strongest moment.

      给自己一晚，知道这个想放弃的念头，让它自己来、自己去。明天再看一次。

      Give it one night. Know the thought of giving up, let it come and go on expansive own. Look again tomorrow.

      动一次，知道一次。这就够了。

      One movement, one knowing. That is enough.

      觉知作为时代焦虑的抵抗Awareness as Resistance to the Anxieties of Our Time

      动中禅修行，及其在 AI 冲击、失业、孤独与意义危机中的位置

      Mahasati practice, and its place amid AI disruption, job loss, loneliness, and the crisis of meaning

      摘要：本文论证，个人持续的禅修练习——具体而言是动中禅（隆波田传承）配合隆波帕默尊者的观心教导——在结构上能够回应当代三种相互交织的焦虑来源：AI 自动化引发的就业不安、流行病学意义上的孤独危机，以及传统意义来源弱化后的存在性空虚。这一论证并非诉诸宗教权威，而是从这三种焦虑各自的结构性根源出发，说明为何一种不依赖外部结果验证的内在练习，能够提供其他应对方式所缺乏的稳定性。

      Abstract: This essay argues that sustained personal meditation practice — specifically Mahasati (Luangpor Teean lineage) alongside Luangpor Pramote's mind-watching teachings — can structurally address three intertwined sources of contemporary anxiety: job insecurity from AI automation, the epidemiological loneliness crisis, and existential emptiness from the erosion of traditional sources of meaning. The argument rests not on religious authority but on the structural roots of each anxiety, showing why a practice independent of external validation offers a stability other coping strategies lack.

      一、问题的提出：三重焦虑的共同结构
      1. Framing the problem: a shared structure across three anxieties

      当代人普遍经历的焦虑，往往被分别讨论——AI 失业焦虑归类为经济议题，孤独归类为公共健康议题，意义感缺失归类为心理或哲学议题。但这三者有一个共同的结构性特征：它们都源于"自我价值依附于外部、不可控、且正在变得更不稳定的事物"。

      Contemporary anxieties are usually discussed separately — AI job anxiety as economic, loneliness as public health, meaninglessness as psychological or philosophical. But all three share a structural feature: self-worth tethered to something external, uncontrollable, and increasingly unstable.

      本文的论证路径是：先分别检视三者的实证证据，再说明为何一种以"觉知本身即是完整的"为核心的修行练习，在结构上能够提供这三重焦虑都缺乏的东西——一个不会因外部结果浮动而跟着浮动的稳定点。

      This essay first examines the evidence behind each, then argues why a practice centered on "awareness itself as already complete" can structurally provide what all three lack: a stable point that doesn't fluctuate with external outcomes.

      二、AI 冲击与就业焦虑的结构
      2. AI disruption and the structure of job anxiety
      证据
      Evidence

      2026 年的数据显示，这种焦虑有充分的现实基础，而非单纯的过度反应。Oracle 在 2026 年成为首家在美国证券交易委员会文件中明确将 AI 列为裁员原因的大型科技公司，当年共削减约 21,000 个职位。截至 2026 年 6 月，已有 267 起裁员事件、累计 185,894 个职位流失，平均每个工作日超过一千人失业，其中 56% 的事件明确提及 AI 或自动化为原因。调查显示，43% 的员工担心使用 AI 反而会让自己的工作变得多余，约一半受访者认为 AI 消灭的工作将多于创造的工作。

      2026 data shows this anxiety is well-founded rather than mere overreaction. Oracle became the first major tech company to name AI as a layoff cause in an SEC filing, cutting roughly 21,000 positions that year. As of June 2026, 267 layoff events had eliminated 185,894 jobs — over 1,000 per working day — with 56% explicitly citing AI or automation. 43% of workers fear AI could make their own jobs redundant, and roughly half believe AI will eliminate more jobs than it creates.

      结构性分析
      Structural analysis

      值得注意的是，这一轮自动化浪潮与历史上的工业自动化有本质差异：以往的机器取代体力劳动，但仍需要大量人力操作，技术本身创造了对其无法替代的人类技能的新需求；而生成式 AI 直接作用于阅读、写作、编程、分析、综合这类"心智劳动"本身。当替代的对象从"双手能做的事"变成"头脑能做的事"，过去那种"被取代的同时又被需要"的补偿机制，是否还能以同样的比例运作，目前并无定论。

      This wave differs fundamentally from past industrial automation: machines once replaced manual labor while still requiring human operators, creating demand for irreplaceable human skills. Generative AI instead acts directly on reading, writing, coding, analysis, and synthesis — "mind work" itself. Whether the old compensation mechanism still holds when what's displaced shifts from "what hands do" to "what minds do" remains unresolved.

      这种不确定性本身，恰恰是焦虑的核心来源：不是"我的工作一定会消失"，而是"我无法确知我赖以建立自我价值的那套能力，会不会在不可预期的时间点突然失去市场价值"。这是一种对自我价值评估标准本身的动摇，而不只是对收入的担忧。

      This uncertainty itself is the core anxiety: not "my job will definitely disappear," but "I cannot know whether the very abilities I built my self-worth on will suddenly lose market value." It's a destabilization of the standard of self-worth, not merely a fear about income.

      三、孤独的流行病学证据
      3. The epidemiological evidence on loneliness
      证据
      Evidence

      美国卫生总署（U.S. Surgeon General）于 2023 年发布的专题报告，将孤独正式定性为公共健康议题，指出社会孤立使过早死亡风险增加 29%，其对死亡率的影响相当于每天吸 15 支香烟，甚至超过肥胖与缺乏运动的影响。报告指出，即便在新冠疫情之前，美国已有近半数成年人报告经历孤独；孤独还使心脏病风险增加 29%、中风风险增加 32%，并使老年群体的失智症风险提高约 50%。另一项研究发现，81% 报告孤独的成年人同时伴有焦虑或抑郁症状，相对未感孤独者的 29%。

      The 2023 U.S. Surgeon General's advisory formally classified loneliness as a public health issue: social isolation raises premature death risk by 29%, a mortality impact comparable to smoking 15 cigarettes a day, exceeding even obesity and physical inactivity. Even pre-pandemic, nearly half of U.S. adults reported loneliness; it raises heart disease risk by 29%, stroke risk by 32%, and dementia risk in older adults by roughly 50%. A separate study found 81% of lonely adults also report anxiety or depression, versus 29% of those not lonely.

      结构性分析
      Structural analysis

      报告中一个关键的概念区分值得注意：社会孤立（social isolation）是客观状态，孤独（loneliness）是主观感受，两者并不等同——有人身边人很多却仍感孤独，因为"不被理解"或"不被欣赏"。这意味着，单纯增加社交数量或被关注的程度（例如直播获得观看），并不必然解决孤独本身；孤独的核心结构，是"实际连接"与"期待中的连接"之间的落差，而这个落差本身，是一种持续变化、依赖他人反应的不稳定状态。

      A key distinction in the report matters here: social isolation is objective, loneliness subjective — they aren't equivalent. Someone surrounded by people can still feel lonely if unappreciated or misunderstood. So simply increasing social quantity or visibility (e.g., livestream viewership) doesn't necessarily resolve loneliness; its core structure is a gap between actual and desired connection — a gap that itself fluctuates, dependent on others' responses.

      四、意义危机：传统意义来源的弱化
      4. The meaning crisis: erosion of traditional sources

      哈佛大学教育学院"关怀共同体"（Making Caring Common）项目的全国调查发现，缺乏意义与目标感，是当代年轻成年人情绪困境的核心驱动因素之一；该调查同时指出技术（73%）、工作过度或疲于奔命（62%）、社会过度个人化（58%）等因素，被受访者普遍认为是孤独与意义缺失的成因。

      A Harvard Graduate School of Education national survey (Making Caring Common) found a lack of meaning and purpose to be a core driver of emotional struggle among young adults, with technology (73%), overwork/exhaustion (62%), and excessive individualism (58%) cited as contributing causes of loneliness and meaninglessness.

      这里的论证关键是：以上三种焦虑——就业、孤独、意义——之所以会同时加剧、彼此强化，是因为现代人高度依赖一组共同的、外部的意义支柱：稳定的职业身份、可预期的社会角色、被他人持续看见和回应。当这组支柱因为 AI、社会结构变迁、技术中介的人际关系而同时变得不稳定，焦虑不会被分散，反而会叠加。

      The key point: these three anxieties intensify together because modern people lean on a shared set of external pillars of meaning — stable career identity, predictable social roles, sustained visibility and response from others. When these pillars all destabilize together — through AI, social change, technologically mediated relationships — anxiety doesn't disperse; it compounds.

      五、觉知练习作为结构性回应
      5. Awareness practice as a structural response
      对就业焦虑：不可自动化的价值基础
      For job anxiety: a value base that cannot be automated

      动中禅训练的核心——对当下身体动作的纯粹觉知——在性质上与可被自动化、可被效率比较的"产出型"能力完全不同。它不产生可被市场定价的输出，因此也不可能被任何模型的进步所淘汰。这并非对就业问题的解决方案，而是一种价值来源上的对冲：如果一个人将全部自我价值都押注在"产出是否还被市场需要"这一件事上，那么这件事的不稳定，会直接转化为自我价值感的不稳定。而若同时持有一个不依赖市场评价的价值来源，外部冲击造成的心理冲击幅度，在结构上会被缓冲。

      Mahasati's core — pure awareness of present bodily movement — is categorically different from output-based skills subject to automation or efficiency comparison. It produces nothing market-priceable, so no model's progress can render it obsolete. This isn't a solution to unemployment, but a hedge in the source of value: if all self-worth rests on whether one's output remains market-needed, that instability transfers directly into self-worth instability. Holding a value source independent of market judgment structurally buffers the psychological impact of external shocks.

      对孤独：处理感受的结构，而非填补连接的数量
      For loneliness: addressing the structure of the feeling, not the quantity of connection

      如前述区分，孤独的核心是"主观感受到的落差"，而不是连接数量的不足。这意味着单纯增加曝光度（例如透过直播被更多人看见）未必能解决孤独，因为落差感可能依然存在，甚至因为期待被进一步推高而扩大。隆波帕默尊者教导的观心法门，处理的恰恰是这个落差感本身——观察"感到孤独"这个念头如何生起、如何变化、如何灭去，而不是依赖外部连接的增加来抵消这种感受。这是对孤独结构本身的直接处理，而非绕过结构、寄望于外部填补。

      As above, loneliness's core is a subjective gap, not a deficit of connection quantity. Increasing exposure (e.g., more livestream viewers) may not resolve it — the gap can persist, even widen as expectations rise. Luangpor Pramote's mind-watching method addresses this gap directly: observing how the thought "I feel lonely" arises, changes, and passes, rather than relying on more external connection to offset the feeling. This treats loneliness's structure directly, rather than bypassing it in hope of external filling.

      对意义危机：一个不会随外部结构崩塌而消失的锚点
      For the meaning crisis: an anchor that doesn't collapse with external structures

      若意义感系于职业身份、社会角色、被持续关注——这些恰恰是本文第二、三节证据显示正在变得不稳定的事物——那么意义感的基础本身就是脆弱的。将修行（灭苦、培养觉知，最终趋向证果）作为意义的主要来源，提供的是一个结构上的优势：它不依赖任何特定的外部结果是否出现，每一次"觉知到一个动作"，作为练习行为本身即已完整，不需要等待订阅数、护持金额、求职结果等外部验证才能成立。这并不是逃避这些现实问题，而是确保自我价值评估不会完全绑定在这些本来就充满不确定性的外部指标上。

      If meaning is tethered to career identity, social roles, sustained attention — precisely what Sections 2 and 3 show destabilizing — its foundation is inherently fragile. Making practice (the cessation of suffering, cultivating awareness, ultimately toward realization) the primary source of meaning offers a structural advantage: it doesn't depend on any specific external outcome. Each act of "knowing a movement" is already complete as practice, with no need to wait for subscriber counts, support, or job outcomes to validate it. This isn't avoidance of real problems — it ensures self-worth isn't entirely bound to inherently uncertain external metrics.

      六、需要诚实指出的局限
      6. Limitations that must be honestly acknowledged

      为使论证保持平衡，几个局限需要诚实承认：

      For a balanced argument, several limitations must be acknowledged:

      • 修行练习不能替代收入。本文第二节所引证据显示的是实际的失业风险，觉知练习能缓冲的是这种风险带来的心理冲击，不能消除风险本身——求职、技能转型等现实行动仍是必要的，不可被修行取代；
      • Practice cannot replace income. The evidence in Section 2 shows real unemployment risk; awareness practice buffers the psychological impact of that risk, not the risk itself — job hunting and reskilling remain necessary and cannot be substituted by practice;
      • 孤独感的处理，临床上仍建议结合实际的人际连接（如卫生总署报告所建议的社会连接框架），单纯的内在观照练习，不应被当作孤独问题的唯一或完整的解法，而是其中一个有证据支持、值得纳入的成分；
      • Clinically, addressing loneliness still recommends real interpersonal connection (per the Surgeon General's social connection framework); inward observation alone shouldn't be treated as the sole or complete solution, but as one evidence-supported component;
      • 公开直播这件事本身，与修行练习的内在逻辑并非完全一致——直播引入了"被看见""被认同"等外部变量，这些变量如果处理不当，反而可能重新引入本文试图缓冲的那种"依赖外部结果"的焦虑结构，需要练习者对此保持自觉。
      • The livestream itself isn't fully consistent with the internal logic of practice — it introduces external variables like "being seen" and "being recognized," which, if mishandled, could reintroduce the very "dependence on external outcomes" anxiety structure this essay tries to buffer. Practitioners must stay self-aware about this.
      结语
      Conclusion

      AI 冲击、孤独、意义危机这三重焦虑，共同的根源是现代生活将自我价值的评估，过度集中地交付给了一组正在变得不稳定的外部结构。觉知练习提供的不是对这些现实问题的解决方案，而是一个结构上独立、不随外部结果浮动的价值锚点——它不能让 AI 不取代某个职位，不能让孤独的人立刻获得理想的陪伴，也不能让求职立刻成功，但它能确保，当这些外部结果不如预期时，一个人的自我价值不会随之整体崩塌。

      AI disruption, loneliness, and the meaning crisis share a root: modern life over-concentrates the evaluation of self-worth into a set of external structures now destabilizing. Awareness practice isn't a solution to these real problems, but a structurally independent value anchor — it can't stop AI from replacing a job, can't give a lonely person ideal companionship, can't make a job search succeed. But it can ensure that when these outcomes disappoint, a person's self-worth doesn't collapse along with them.

      在一个意义来源加速不稳定的时代，拥有至少一个不会动摇的锚点，本身就是一种切实的、可论证的抵抗。

      In an age where sources of meaning are destabilizing faster, holding at least one unshakeable anchor is itself a real, arguable form of resistance.
