这是什么What this is

动中禅（Mahasati），是已故泰国禅师隆波田所传授的禅修方法，透过一套规律的手部动作，培养对身体动作的纯粹觉知。练习时不需默念、不需分析，只是单纯地"动一次，知道一次"。

Mahasati, taught by the late Thai meditation master Luangpor Teean, is a practice of cultivating pure awareness of bodily movement through a set sequence of hand motions. No mantra, no analysis — simply moving, and knowing that you moved.

这个法门强调自然、轻松、不刻意，可以与日常生活结合，随时随地练习。直播的目的是借助"被记录、被看见"这件事，督促自己长期、规律地坚持练习——不是表演，也不是教学。直播间里呈现的是真实的练习过程：安静、重复，有专注，也会有妄念生起，如实呈现，不剪辑、不美化。

The method emphasizes naturalness and ease over forced concentration, and can be practiced anywhere, integrated into daily life. The livestream exists to use accountability — being seen, being recorded — as a tool for sustaining long-term, regular practice. It is not a performance or a teaching session. What you'll see is the real process: quiet, repetitive, sometimes focused, sometimes wandering — shown as it is, unedited and unembellished.

全程安静练习，不讲解、不开示。不进行宗教比较、辩论或劝说，也不代表任何宗教立场的评判。

The practice is silent throughout — no narration, no teaching. This is not a space for religious comparison, debate, or persuasion, and does not represent a judgment of any other faith.

常见问题
FAQ

常见问题Frequently asked

为何不出家？Why not become a monk?

出家是一种选择，不是唯一正确的修行方式。我评估过实际情况——过午不食的身体限制，以及寺院日常的法会与接待活动对我而言负荷过高——目前在家修行更适合我。出家不是"更高级"的修行，重点是找到自己能持续走下去的方式。

Ordination is one path, not the only valid one. I've weighed the realities — my body cannot sustain the no-food-after-noon rule, and monastery life's frequent ceremonies and hosting duties are more than I can sustain. Lay practice isn't a lesser form — what matters is finding a form that's actually sustainable.

为何无法守八戒？Why can't you keep the Eight Precepts?

过午不食这一条，我身体状况无法配合，不是不愿意。戒律的精神是简化生活、减少贪欲，这个精神我在日常生活中以适合自己身体状况的方式实践，但不勉强做身体无法承受的事——那会让修行变成跟身体对抗。

The no-food-after-noon rule specifically conflicts with my body's needs — this isn't unwillingness. I practice the spirit of simplicity and reduced craving in ways my body can sustain, rather than forcing a rule that would turn practice into a fight against my own body.

为何要公开禅修？Why livestream meditation publicly?

公开直播是为了督促自己长期坚持练习，不是为了表演或弘法。动中禅本身不需要观众，闭门练也完全成立——公开只是给自己多一点外部督促力。

The livestream is an accountability tool for sustaining long-term practice — not a performance or outreach effort. The practice itself needs no audience; going public simply adds a layer of external accountability for myself.

为何用动中禅，而不是其他方法？Why this method, not another?

我接触过不同的禅修方式，最终在隆波田、隆波帕默的动中禅传承上持续了近五年。选择一个法门不是"哪个最正统"的比较，是"哪个我真的能长期稳定地练下去"——这是我自己走了五年验证过的。

I've explored different approaches and have sustained the Mahasati lineage of Luangpor Teean and Luangpor Pramote for nearly five years. Choosing a method isn't about which is most orthodox — it's about which one is actually sustainable, and this is the one I've verified through five years of practice.

为何不去找正式工作？Why not get a regular job?

我一直在积极求职，方向是自动化与 AI 相关岗位，这跟我的学历和技术背景匹配。直播禅修和求职是同时进行的两件事，不是用禅修取代工作。

I am actively job hunting, focused on automation and AI-related roles aligned with my background. The livestream and job search happen in parallel — meditation is not a substitute for work.

为何依赖家人？Why rely on family support?

这是过渡期的暂时安排。我有自己的存款,足够支撑至少半年的生活，家人的支持是额外缓冲，不是唯一来源，也不是长期依赖。

This is a temporary arrangement during a transition period. I have personal savings covering at least six months; family support is an additional buffer, not the sole or permanent source.

这样做是不是在逃避现实？Isn't this just avoidance?

我同时在积极求职，这不是逃避，是选择用这段过渡期同时安顿自己的身心状态。

I'm actively job hunting in parallel — this isn't avoidance, it's choosing to also tend to my wellbeing during a transitional period.
